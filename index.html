Privacy & Security

Zero logging. Period. We don't track, store, or sell your DNS queries. Unlike your ISP or free DNS services, we have no interest in your browsing data. We query root servers directly, ensuring complete independence from legacy provider companies.

Trust is earned, not claimed. Here's our approach to transparency:

  • 💰 We have no business model requiring your data (you pay us directly)
  • 🔧 Our infrastructure is designed to process queries in memory without persistent storage
  • 🏢 We're not a venture-backed company with investors demanding data monetization
  • 📊 We publish regular transparency reports about our operations

Unlike "free" services that must monetize users somehow, our $3/month fee eliminates any incentive to collect or sell your data.

SiftDNS prevents your ISP from seeing DNS queries, but they can still see:

  • 🌐 IP addresses you connect to (though not what you do there)
  • 📊 Amount of data transferred
  • ⏰ Connection timestamps

For complete privacy from your ISP, combine SiftDNS with a VPN. SiftDNS hides your browsing intentions at the DNS level, while a VPN encrypts all traffic. This combination provides maximum privacy and ad blocking.

Note: HTTPS websites (which most sites use) already encrypt content from your ISP, but DNS queries reveal your intentions before you connect.

Since we don't store your browsing data, there's nothing to compromise if we shut down. The worst case scenario:

  • 📱 Your devices would fall back to default DNS (no data breach risk)
  • 🚫 You'd lose ad blocking until switching to another service
  • 🔒 No personal information would be exposed because we don't collect it

This is the advantage of our privacy-first approach – your data can't be compromised because we don't have it to begin with.
